unmonastery.org

A social clinic for the future, rethinking monasticism and hackerspaces to prototype a network of spaces throughout Europe. unMonastery addresses the interlinked needs of empty space, unemployment and depleting social services by embedding committed, skilled individuals within communities that could benefit from their presence.
